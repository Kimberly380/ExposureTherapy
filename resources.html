Resources

The following links pertain to phobias and phobia treatment. We encourage you to consult them prior to beginning the Conquer It! program to better understand what phobias are and what treatment options are available.

  • AADA Specific Phobias - An explanation of specific phobias from the Anxiety and Depression Association of America (ADAA)
  • The Biology of Phobia - Information about phobia research from the American Psychological Association
  • The Fear Factor: Phobias - Phobia information and treatment options from WebMD
  • FindAPsychologist.org - Search for psychologists by location and area of expertise
  • Support Groups - Search ADAA's database for support groups near you or start your own
  • Phobia Workbooks - Amazon.com's selection of phobia workbooks
